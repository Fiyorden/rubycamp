The Concept

It's unformal and community based. You want to give a workshop or a talk? Do it. You want to build a project with others? Do it. This is also a good time for uncoding activities (like sports!) and for knowing each other better. It comes from the original RailsCamp.

Code Of Conduct

We are dedicated to provide a harassment-free camp for everyone. We do not tolerate harassment and unappropriate behavior. We follow the same Code of Conduct than Arrrrcamp.

The package

The price of 100€ includes 2 nights, food, soft-drinks and light beers, and a membership to the new association Ruby Belgium.

If you don't sleep over, it's 75€. If you come only one day, it's 35€. If you join us only for Friday evening, it's 15€. In those packages, the membership to the Ruby Belgium association is not included.

Organizers

The event is organized by Christophe and Yannick.

When and Where

It is in Tilff (check the map below) during the last weekend of August 2015. From Friday evening the 28th August to Sunday afternoon the 30th August.

The venue

We will spend the week-end on a beautiful castle, featuring all the necessities for indoor as well as outdoors activities.

From the coding sessions to the sports and outside leisure, everything is in place to spend a great time together.

Tilff is accessible by bike, train, bus, or car.
